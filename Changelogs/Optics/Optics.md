# Persephone 3 Pro Optics

Completely DiY Optical solution for the Persephone 3 Pro Headset

## Lense Setup no.1

### Lense "stackup"

Optical "stackup" consists of two fresnel lenses per eye.

 - 70mm diameter lense with 30mm focal lenght
 - 70mm diameter lense with 40mm focal lenght
 
 Complete lense "stack" has focal lenght at about 14.7mm and thickness at about 3mm

### Lense "trim"

Each lense has to be trimmed down to correct shape.

Lense center has to be directly in front of the eye. That's why bigger diameter lenses are used.

Trim was designed in such a way to remove as much plastic as possible, without constraining visibility.

Current stack makes it possible to cover almost whole human FOV. (I have literally one set of eyes in my head so I can't verify this)
